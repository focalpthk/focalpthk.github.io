What is focalpt.?

focalpt. is originated from an optical term – focal point – when an object is focused, its light rays are converged at the focal point and that is when you see a sharp image.

I chose this term as the title of my album (or portfolio) because I hope my creations could bring you the focus of certain issues and echoes my physics study.

From physics to taking pictures?

My mother bought me my first camera for astrophotography in Form 4, that time I was the chairperson of astronomy club, this triggered my interest into astronomy and photography.

Photography and physics are different, I remembered in Year 3, I found passion in taking photos of the world, the humanity living between light and shadow. I struggled a lot when I asked myself if I would continue my Physics study in the future or develop a photography career. This question led me to study a portraiture photography course offered by my university.

During this period of study (2019), Hong Kong was under a very intense situation of fighting for freedom regarded to the introduction of extradition bill. I took the chance to document this historical movement since I decided to stand aside as an observer and hoped I could bring myself and others different angles of the movement. At the same time, I joined university's Editorial Board as a volunteer journalist, stand up for justice and freedom.

The course and being a journalist then became a revelation of what should I focus after my graduation. I understood this transformation will be a hard time for me, but I hope I can follow my passion and make greater things for the world.
